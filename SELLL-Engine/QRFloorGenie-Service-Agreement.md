## 1. SERVICES

### 1.1 Scope of Services

SELLL agrees to provide the following GTM and revenue engine services
("Services") for the Client's product, QR Floor Genie:

**(a) Signal Layer Build**
Design and deployment of a five-trigger signal monitoring system
targeting the Client's ideal customer profile (independent flooring
store owners in the United States), including:
- New store opening detection
- Showroom manager hire monitoring
- Negative Google review signal tracking (pricing-related)
- Trade show attendance identification
- New website launch monitoring

**(b) Outbound Infrastructure**
Setup and management of email sending infrastructure, including:
- 15 sending domains (purchased via Porkbun, costs billed to Client)
- 15 mailboxes via PremiumInbox (costs billed to Client)
- Warmup protocol across 3 batches over Weeks 1, 3, and 5
- 10 active mailboxes + 5 backup mailboxes in rotation

**(c) Sequence Development**
Writing and deployment of five (5) signal-specific outbound sequences
(one per trigger event), covering email, cold calling guidance,
and LinkedIn touchpoints.

**(d) SDR Deployment**
Assignment of one (1) dedicated Sales Development Representative (SDR)
to the Client's account, responsible for:
- Signal-triggered cold calling (primary channel)
- Email sequence execution and monitoring
- LinkedIn outreach support
- Weekly pipeline reporting to Client

**(e) Reporting**
Weekly reporting delivered to Client, covering:
- Signals detected and sequences fired
- Calls made, connect rate, and conversations held
- Emails sent and reply rate
- Qualified demos booked
- Pipeline value generated

**(f) Ongoing Optimisation (Months 1-3)**
Continuous sequence refinement based on performance data, including
subject line testing, call script iteration, and signal prioritisation
based on conversion data.

### 1.2 Client Tool Requirements

Client is responsible for maintaining and providing access to:
- Apollo.io account (existing)
- Smartlead account (existing)
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator account (existing)

Client is responsible for purchasing and funding:
- 15 sending domains via Porkbun (~$225 one-time)
- 15 PremiumInbox mailboxes (~$75/month)
- Clay subscription ($149-349/month, tier to be agreed at kickoff)

### 1.3 SELLL Tool Stack

The following tools are operated by SELLL and covered within the
monthly retainer fee at no additional cost to Client:
Trigify, RB2B Pro, GetSales, Claude API, Attio CRM,
Millionverifier, Taplio, Sendspark, PhantomBuster, n8n,
Crunchbase, Fathom, Notion. SELLL reserves the right to

## 3. PERFORMANCE GUARANTEE

### 3.1 Month 1 Guarantee

SELLL guarantees a minimum of three (3) qualified demo meetings
booked for the Client within the first thirty (30) days of active
outbound ("Month 1 Guarantee"). A "qualified demo" is defined as
a booked meeting with a US-based independent flooring store owner
or decision-maker who has expressed interest in QR Floor Genie.

### 3.2 Remedy for Underperformance

If SELLL fails to deliver three (3) or more qualified demos by
Day 30 of active outbound, Month 2 of the Agreement will be
provided at no charge to the Client. SELLL will continue to
perform Services during Month 2 without invoicing until the
Month 1 Guarantee target is met.

### 3.3 Guarantee Conditions

The Month 1 Guarantee is contingent upon:
(a) Client completing setup of required tool accounts within
    5 business days of the Effective Date
(b) Client providing timely responses to SELLL communications
    (within 2 business days)
(c) Client not materially changing the ICP or target market
    after Week 1 of the Pilot Period
(d) SELLL having full access to agreed tools and platforms
    from Day 1 of active outbound

## 4. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Term

This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues
on a month-to-month basis following the Pilot Period, unless
terminated in accordance with this Section.

### 4.2 Termination by Either Party

Either Party may terminate this Agreement with thirty (30) days'
written notice to the other Party. Written notice may be delivered
via email to the contact addresses above.

### 4.3 Termination During Pilot Period

During the Pilot Period (Months 1-3), the Client may terminate
with thirty (30) days' written notice. In the event of termination
during the Pilot Period, all fees accrued and due prior to the
termination date remain payable. No partial-month refunds are issued
unless covered by Section 3.2 (Performance Guarantee).

### 4.4 Termination for Cause

Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written
notice if the other Party:
(a) Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such
    breach within 10 business days of written notice; or
(b) Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy protection.

### 4.5 Effect of Termination

Upon termination, SELLL will:
- Cease all outbound activity on behalf of Client
- Deliver a final performance report within 5 business days
- Transfer ownership of all Client-specific contact lists
  and sequence copy to Client upon receipt of all outstanding fees
