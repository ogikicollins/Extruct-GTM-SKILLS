## Why No-Shows Happen

1. They forgot (no reminder or reminder too late)
2. They de-prioritised (something came up and they had no reason to reschedule)
3. They got cold feet (the gap between "I'm interested" and the call gave them time to second-guess)
4. They were never that interested (low-quality booking — wrong ICP, vague CTA)

The fix is a reinforced reminder chain that keeps the meeting top of mind, builds anticipation, and makes rescheduling easy so you recover no-shows instead of losing them.

---

## Pre-Call Reminder Chain (Automated + Manual)

### Immediately on booking (automated — Calendly):
- Confirmation email with: date, time, Calendly link to reschedule, Kaya/Collins name, one sentence on what to expect on the call

### 24 hours before (automated — Calendly or HubSpot):
- Email reminder: "Your call with ALIF is tomorrow at [time]. If anything's changed just reschedule here: [link]"

### Day of, 1 hour before (manual — Collins or Amir):

**WhatsApp (MENA prospects):**
"Hey [First Name] — Collins here from ALIF. Quick reminder we're speaking in an hour. Looking forward to it. [Calendly link if they need to reschedule]"

**Email (EU/US prospects):**
Subject: "See you in an hour, [First Name]"
Body: "Just a quick note — we're on for [time] today. See you then. Collins, ALIF Agency"

### Day of, 15 minutes before (manual — if no response to 1h message):
**LinkedIn DM (if connected):**
"[First Name] — Collins from ALIF. On shortly — see you in 15."

## No-Show Response Protocol

**The moment a meeting is missed (within 5 minutes of start time):**

Step 1 — WhatsApp (MENA) or email (EU/US):
"Hey [First Name] — Collins here. We were scheduled for right now — looks like there may have been a mix-up. Completely fine. Are you still free to jump on? Or happy to find another time."

Step 2 — If no response after 30 minutes, call them:
Use Script 3 (No-Show Recovery) from the Cold Call Scripts doc.

Step 3 — If no answer, leave voicemail + LinkedIn DM:
**Voicemail:** "[First Name], Collins from ALIF — we had a call just now and I think something came up. No problem at all — give me a ring back when you're free or just book a new slot: [Calendly link]. Speak soon."
**LinkedIn DM:** "Hey [First Name] — just tried calling. Wanted to make sure you had a way to rebook easily: [Calendly link]. No pressure — just don't want the timing to stop us connecting."

Step 4 — If no response after 24 hours:
Send the re-engagement email:
Subject: "Still worth a conversation?"
"Hey [First Name], we missed each other yesterday — I'm guessing something came up. Completely understand. Still think what I wanted to show you is relevant to [company name]. Worth finding another 20 minutes? Here's my calendar: [Calendly link]. If timing's off altogether, just let me know and I'll leave you alone. Collins"

Step 5 — Log in HubSpot as "No Show — Rebook Attempted." If no response within 7 days, move to 60-day nurture sequence.

## No-Show Prevention: The Pre-Call Asset System

**On every booking, trigger these Asana tasks automatically (via HubSpot → Zapier → Asana):**

- [ ] Send prospect a loom or short video recap of what ALIF does (2 min max) — makes them feel invested before the call
- [ ] Pull their company's Instagram + last 3 ads (if running) — so Collins has specifics ready
- [ ] Check their website PageSpeed score and mobile score
- [ ] Find their most recent LinkedIn post — reference it in the first 60 seconds of the call
- [ ] Prepare 1 specific observation about their brand to open with

**Why this matters for no-shows:** When a prospect has received personalised pre-call prep from you (a Loom, a specific observation), the psychological cost of ghosting goes up. They feel the relationship has started.

## MENA-Specific Note

For UAE/Saudi/MENA prospects: WhatsApp is the primary channel for reminders. Do not rely on email. A WhatsApp message from a real person (not a bot) 1 hour before the call is the highest-performing show-up trigger in the region.

If you have their WhatsApp number — use it. If you don't — get it during the booking confirmation by adding a custom Calendly question: "What's the best WhatsApp number to reach you on the day?"
