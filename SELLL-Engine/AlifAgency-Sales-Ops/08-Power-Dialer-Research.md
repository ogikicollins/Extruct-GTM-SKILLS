## The Requirement

HubSpot Pro includes a built-in dialer but it's single-call only — you dial one number at a time, manually. For cold calling at volume (target: 50–80 calls/day), we need a power dialer that:
- Automatically dials the next number the moment a call ends
- Logs calls and dispositions directly into HubSpot
- Records calls for coaching
- Works for UAE/MENA numbers (international calling)
- Ideally has a local presence number for MENA (UAE +971 prefix increases answer rates)

---

## Options Evaluated

**Best overall for ALIF**

- **Key features:**
  - Auto-dialer + power dialer (skips no-answers automatically)
  - Native HubSpot integration (2-way sync — logs calls, dispositions, recordings)
  - Local UAE numbers available (+971)
  - WhatsApp integration (relevant for MENA follow-up)

  - Call recording + AI call summaries
- **MENA suitability:** Strong — UAE, Saudi, Egypt numbers available
- **Verdict:** Recommended. HubSpot-native, UAE numbers, WhatsApp in one tool.
