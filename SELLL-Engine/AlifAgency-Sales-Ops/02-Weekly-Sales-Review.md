## Weekly Sales Review — Agenda (60 min)

### 1. Last Week Numbers (10 min)
Collins presents the metrics scorecard — no discussion yet, just the numbers.

### 2. Win/Loss Analysis (15 min)
- What closed last week? Why did it close?
- What was lost? Why was it lost?
- One specific thing we learned from each.

### 3. Pipeline Review (15 min)
- Walk every open deal that is in Discovery Scheduled or later.
- For each: what's the next action, who owns it, by when?
- Flag any deal that hasn't moved in 7+ days — make a decision (push, nurture, or cut).

### 4. This Week's Priorities (10 min)
- Collins sets 3 specific targets for the week.
- Agreement from Kaya on any decisions Collins needs to make things move.

### 5. Process / Automation Updates (10 min)
- What SOPs or automations were built or improved last week?
- What's next on the build list?

## Daily Sync Agenda (30 min)

**Structure:**

**Yesterday (5 min)**
- What happened? Any replies, calls, closes?
- Any blockers that came up?

**Today (10 min)**
- Collins: what are the 3 most important actions today?
- Hot lead review: anyone who replied in last 24h who needs attention?
- Any calls scheduled today — review prospect context together

**Decisions needed from Kaya (10 min)**
- Anything Collins needs sign-off on to move a deal forward
- Pricing exceptions, proposal approvals, intro requests

**Build/ops update (5 min)**
- Any automations or SOPs updated
- Any issues with Apex or the stack
