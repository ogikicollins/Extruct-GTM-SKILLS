## 1. Company Overview

QR Floor Genie was founded by someone who spent 25+ years inside the flooring industry and watched the same problems repeat themselves: outdated price binders, manual updates, misquotes, lost margins, and customers walking out because they couldn't get a straight answer on price.

The product automates the entire pricing layer of a flooring showroom. Live manufacturer feeds push price updates directly into the system. An AI layer reads and maps price sheets that don't come in a standard format. Every physical flooring sample on the showroom floor gets a QR label. A customer or staff member scans the code → instant product details, price per square foot, availability, and comparison data on their phone.

Secondary capabilities include lead capture (every scan can capture contact information), analytics on most-scanned products (showing which samples generate the most interest), and CRM/Excel export for sales follow-up.

**Three domains are associated with the product:**
- qrfloorgenie.com (primary)
- qfloorgenie.com (redirect/alternate)
- floorgenieqr.com (collection/catalog page)

This multi-domain presence suggests the brand is still being refined.
