## RECITALS

SELLL.io provides signal-triggered outbound revenue generation services to B2B companies. Client wishes to engage SELLL.io to design, build, and operate a revenue generation system targeting independent flooring retailers in the United States on Client's behalf.

The Parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. SERVICES

### 1.1 Scope of Services

During the Term, SELLL.io shall provide the following services to Client (collectively, the "Services"):

**(a) Signal Architecture and Monitoring**
Design and operation of a five-trigger signal monitoring system targeting Client's Ideal Customer Profile (independent US flooring retailers), including: new store openings, showroom manager hires, negative Google reviews mentioning pricing, trade show attendance, and new website launches. Signal monitoring shall run continuously throughout the Term.

**(b) Lead Identification and Enrichment**
Identification of flooring store owner contacts corresponding to signal trigger events. Enrichment of each contact with verified email address, phone number, LinkedIn profile, and firmographic data using third-party tools including Apollo.io and Clay.

**(c) Outbound Sequence Design and Operation**
Creation of five (5) trigger-specific outbound email sequences and accompanying cold call scripts. Sequences shall be personalized to the specific trigger event that generated each lead. Email sequences shall be operated via Smartlead or equivalent email sequencing platform. All sequence copy shall be submitted to Client for review and approval prior to launch. Client approval shall not be unreasonably withheld or delayed beyond 48 hours of submission.

**(d) Cold Calling**
Signal-triggered cold calling to flooring store owner prospects by a trained SELLL.io SDR using a power dialer with local US area code numbers. Calls shall reference the specific trigger event where applicable. All calls shall be recorded and available to Client upon request. SELLL.io shall provide a minimum of 100 cold call attempts per month to trigger-qualified prospects during the active service period.

**(e) LinkedIn Outreach**
Pre-engagement and connection campaigns via LinkedIn Automation (Expandi or equivalent), operating within LinkedIn's stated usage policies. LinkedIn outreach shall serve as a support channel behind email and cold calling, not as a primary channel.

**(f) CRM and Pipeline Management**
Configuration and maintenance of a HubSpot Starter pipeline (or equivalent CRM agreed upon by both Parties) tracking all leads, sequence activity, call outcomes, and demo bookings attributable to SELLL.io activity.

**(g) Weekly Reporting**
Delivery of a weekly performance report to Client via email or shared Google Sheets dashboard, covering: signal volume by trigger type, emails sent, calls made, reply rates, demo bookings, and pipeline status.

### 1.2 Onboarding and Launch Timeline

SELLL.io shall use commercially reasonable efforts to complete system build and launch outreach within fourteen (14) calendar days of receipt of the items listed in Section 1.3 below.

### 1.3 Client Obligations

Client shall provide the following within two (2) business days of the Effective Date, or the launch timeline shall be adjusted accordingly:

(a) Access to any existing Apollo.io account and/or prospect lists held by Client;
(b) A functional demo booking link (Calendly or equivalent) with a minimum of ten (10) available time slots per calendar week throughout Month 1;
(c) Purchase of a dedicated outbound sending domain (SELLL.io will provide domain specifications; Client owns the domain);
(d) A product demonstration recording (Loom, Zoom recording, or equivalent) sufficient for SELLL.io SDR product training;
(e) Written approval of all sequence copy within 48 hours of submission by SELLL.io;
(f) Prompt response to any booked demos — Client agrees to confirm attendance within 24 hours of a demo booking notification.

Failure by Client to fulfill the obligations in this Section 1.3 within the specified timeframes shall toll the launch timeline and any applicable performance guarantee periods by the number of days of delay attributable to Client.

## 2. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 2.1 Initial Pilot Term

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a minimum period of three (3) calendar months (the "Pilot Term"), unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Section 2.

### 2.2 Continuation

Following the Pilot Term, this Agreement shall continue on a month-to-month basis at the rates set forth in Section 3.2 until either Party provides written notice of termination in accordance with Section 2.3.

### 2.3 Termination for Convenience

Either Party may terminate this Agreement after the Pilot Term by providing thirty (30) calendar days' written notice to the other Party via email. Termination notice must be delivered to the email address specified in the Parties section of this Agreement.

### 2.4 Termination for Cause

Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party:

(a) Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within ten (10) calendar days after receiving written notice of the breach;
(b) Becomes insolvent, makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors, or has a receiver or administrator appointed;
(c) Engages in fraudulent or illegal conduct materially related to the services under this Agreement.

### 2.5 Effect of Termination

Upon termination, SELLL.io shall: (i) cease all outbound activity on Client's behalf within two (2) business days; (ii) transfer to Client all sequence copy, contact lists sourced using Client's budget, and domain credentials; (iii) provide a final performance report. Fees accrued through the date of termination are due and payable within seven (7) calendar days of termination.

## 4. PERFORMANCE GUARANTEE

### 4.1 Month 1 Demo Guarantee

SELLL.io guarantees delivery of a minimum of three (3) Qualified Demos (as defined in Section 4.2) during Month 1 of the Pilot Term.

### 4.2 Definition of Qualified Demo

A "Qualified Demo" means: a scheduled, held video call or phone call of minimum fifteen (15) minutes in duration between a flooring store owner or authorized decision-maker at an independent US flooring retailer and the Client's designated representative, during which QR Floor Genie's product is demonstrated. A booked but not held demo does not count as a Qualified Demo.

### 4.3 Remedy for Non-Performance

If SELLL.io delivers fewer than three (3) Qualified Demos during Month 1 of the Pilot Term, SELLL.io shall provide Month 2 Services at no charge to Client. Month 2 services under this remedy provision shall continue until either:

(a) The cumulative Qualified Demo count reaches three (3) (counting demos booked in Month 2); or
(b) Month 2 ends, whichever occurs first.

If the combined Month 1 and Month 2 Qualified Demo count remains below three (3) at the end of Month 2, the Parties shall negotiate in good faith a mutually acceptable resolution.

### 4.4 Guarantee Conditions

The Performance Guarantee in Section 4.1 applies only if ALL of the following conditions are met by Client throughout Month 1:

(a) Client maintains a minimum of ten (10) available demo booking slots per calendar week in the booking platform;
(b) Client confirms or declines each demo booking notification within twenty-four (24) hours of receipt;
(c) Client's product demonstration is functional and capable of being shown to prospects throughout Month 1;
(d) Client approves sequence copy submitted by SELLL.io within 48 hours of submission;
(e) Client has fulfilled all obligations under Section 1.3 within the timeframes specified.

Failure by Client to meet any of the conditions in this Section 4.4 shall toll the guarantee period by the number of days the condition remains unmet. SELLL.io shall notify Client in writing within 48 hours of identifying a guarantee condition that is not being met.

### 4.5 Limitation of Guarantee

The Performance Guarantee is SELLL.io's sole and exclusive remedy obligation relating to demo volume. SELLL.io does not guarantee conversion rates, signed contracts, revenue, or any outcome dependent on Client's sales process after the demo is booked.
