## BUILD PLAN WALKTHROUGH — MINUTES 2-17

Walk through QRFloorGenie-Build-Plan.md on screen share.

### STOP 1 — THE SIGNAL ARCHITECTURE (5 min)

**"The core difference between what you've been running on Apollo
and what we're building is this: your current setup treats every
flooring store on the list equally. It doesn't know who just opened
last week, who just hired a showroom manager, who just got a
bad Google review about pricing confusion."**

**"We do. And we fire a sequence at them within 24 hours of
that event. That's why your reply rate goes from 1-2% to 4-6%."**

Walk through each signal. Pause on Signal 1 (New Store Opening) and Signal 2 (Showroom Manager Hire) — these are the most visceral for James.

**"The new store opening signal is your best one. A store owner
in week one has no vendor locked in for pricing. That window
is roughly 30 days before they find a workaround.
Right now, nobody is calling those stores when they open."**

---

### STOP 2 — COLD CALLING AS PRIMARY (2 min)

**"You told me on the last call you're getting a 15-16% connect
rate on cold calls. That's actually really strong for this ICP."**

**"The problem isn't the channel. It's the targeting. When an SDR
calls a store that just opened last Tuesday and leads with
'we saw your store just opened' — that connect becomes a conversation.
When they call from a random list — it becomes a hang-up."**

**"Signal + cold call is the combination that books demos for
your type of ICP."**

---

### STOP 3 — THE TIMELINE (3 min)

Walk through the month-by-month plan:

**"Week 1 is infrastructure — domains, warmup, sequences written,
signal monitoring live. Week 2 the SDR starts dialing."**

**"By Day 30: 3-4 demos. That's the guarantee."**

**"Month 2 we optimise on what worked. Month 3 we scale.
By Month 3 you're looking at 6-8 demos per month."**

**"Month 4 — you're at $2,500 a month. The SDR is trained,
the machine is running, and you're deciding whether you want
them booking or closing."**

---

### STOP 4 — PIPELINE MATH (2 min)

**"Here's what Month 1 looks like on paper — and I want
to be conservative here, not optimistic."**

**"150 calls, 15-16% connect rate — that's 22-23 conversations.
Of those, 30-40% become a demo request. That's 6-9 conversations
that turn into 3-4 booked demos."**

**"That is the guarantee number. Not the ceiling."**

right now."**
