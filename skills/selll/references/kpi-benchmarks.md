# SELLL KPI Benchmarks

Reference this file when interpreting campaign results in `revenue-reporting`, evaluating hypothesis performance, and setting client expectations.

---

## Outreach Benchmarks

| Metric | SELLL Target | Industry Average | Red Line (investigate) |
|--------|-------------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Email open rate | 35–55% | 20–30% | < 15% (deliverability issue) |
| Email reply rate | 3–5% | 0.5–1% | < 0.5% (targeting or message issue) |
| Positive reply rate | ≥ 30% of all replies | 15–20% | < 10% (message-market fit issue) |
| Bounce rate | < 2% | 3–5% | > 5% (list quality issue) |
| Unsubscribe rate | < 0.5% | 0.2–1% | > 1% (targeting issue) |
| LinkedIn connection accept rate | 30–50% | 20–30% | < 15% (profile trust issue) |
| LinkedIn DM reply rate | 10–20% | 5–10% | < 5% (message issue) |

## Hypothesis Performance Thresholds

| Verdict | Reply Rate | Positive Reply Rate | Min Sends |
|---------|-----------|---------------------|-----------|
| ✅ Active (keep running) | ≥ 3% | ≥ 30% of replies | ≥ 30 |
| 🔄 Refine (adjust message) | 1–3% | 15–30% | ≥ 30 |
| ❌ Retire (pause + replace) | < 1% | < 15% | ≥ 50 |

Do not retire a hypothesis until it has at least 50 sends. Sample size matters.

## Deliverability Health Thresholds

| Signal | Healthy | Warning | Critical |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| Open rate | 35%+ | 20–35% | < 15% |
| Bounce rate | < 2% | 2–4% | > 5% |
| Spam complaint rate | < 0.1% | 0.1–0.3% | > 0.3% |
| Reply-to-send ratio | > 2% | 0.5–2% | < 0.5% |

If any signal hits Critical: pause campaign immediately, audit sending infrastructure.

## A/B Test Minimums

Run A/B tests on subject lines and opening lines only. Requirements:
- Minimum 50 sends per variant before declaring a winner
- Test one element at a time (subject OR opening line, never both)
- Measure: subject line → open rate; opening line → reply rate
- 95% confidence threshold before changing default
