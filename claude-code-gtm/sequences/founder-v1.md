> Variant: `Founder_v1`
> Persona: Persona 2 — CEO / Founder / Co-Founder
> Trigger: H3 signal | Founder confirmed in active selling role | No VP Sales or VP Sales < 90 days with founder still involved
> Emails: 5 | Duration: 22 days
> Sending name: Aaron Shepard (always — founder-to-founder is the only frame that works here)

---

## What This Sequence Is Doing

The founder is the most difficult persona to convert and the most powerful buyer in the system. They built the company by selling. They are likely better at it than anyone they've hired. And they know — with uncomfortable clarity — that this won't scale.

The problem is that every time they try to hand off sales, something breaks. So they take it back. And the cycle repeats.

They're not looking for another salesperson or another tool. They've tried both. What they need — and haven't been able to articulate — is a system that captures how THEY sell and makes it repeatable without them in the room.

The founder sequence never pitches. It mirrors. Every email reflects an observation about the founder's situation that they've thought themselves but never seen written down. When that happens, the reply rate from this persona is among the highest we see.

**Non-negotiable rule:** This sequence is always sent from Aaron's name. Founder-to-founder is the only frame that earns the right to speak to this persona. A cold email from an SDR to a founder about founder-led sales will not land.

## SDR Operating Notes

**This sequence is sent from Aaron's name exclusively.**
**SDR role: execute logistics, monitor replies, escalate everything to Aaron.**

**Finding the right H3 signal:**
- Founder appears in LinkedIn posts about customer meetings or deals won (they're still in the room)
- No VP Sales found on LinkedIn company page (or VP Sales has been there < 90 days)
- Company > 25 employees with no dedicated sales leader = almost always H3
- Company page shows the CEO/Founder with a sales-adjacent title (Head of Growth, Chief Revenue Officer for a < 30 person company)

**How to fill `{{proof_outcome}}` for this persona:**
- Proof outcome must reference the founder's experience, not just the company's metrics
- Format: "Founder stepped out of day-to-day selling by week 6. Pipeline continued without him."
- Metrics are secondary to the founder's freedom — lead with the freedom, follow with the number

**If the founder replies defensively ("I like being in deals"):**
→ "That makes complete sense — most of the founders I work with say the same thing at the start. What they want is the choice, not the removal." (Route per reply-routing.md → Objection 12)

**If the founder replies with a genuine question about the process:**
→ This is a HOT reply. Alert Aaron within 2 hours. Aaron handles all conversations from here.
