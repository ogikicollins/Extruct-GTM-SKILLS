## Rules

- Never send a referral ask without founder review — these are relationship emails
- Best ask moment: Day 30–45 (first results delivered). Never ask in first 30 days.
- Maximum: once every 60 days per client
- Referrals skip Layers 2–4 — route directly to Layer 5 (deal nurture) via `account-research` first
- Always get 4 intake answers before first outreach: (1) what did you tell them, (2) what pain, (3) how well do you know them, (4) are they expecting my call
