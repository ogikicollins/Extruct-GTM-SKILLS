## Outcome Definitions

| Outcome | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| **MEETING BOOKED** | Contact booked a call — re-engagement worked |
| **WARM REPLY** | Replied positively but hasn't booked — continue nurture |
| **NOT NOW (AGAIN)** | Deferred again — re-suppress, set a new trigger watch |
| **HARD NO** | Now a hard no — move to DNC, remove from watchlist |
| **NO REPLY** | 3-email sequence complete, no response — move to fatigue-suppressed |
| **REFERRAL** | Redirected us to another contact — open new account-research |

## Target Benchmarks

- Reply rate: 40–60% of fresh cold outreach reply rate
- Meeting rate from re-engagement: 15–25%
- Pipeline contribution from re-engagement: 15–25% of total
- Time trigger → outreach sent: < 24h (CRITICAL), < 72h (HIGH)
