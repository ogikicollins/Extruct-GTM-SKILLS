## When to Send a Proposal

Send ONLY when ALL of these are true:

```
□ Discovery call completed (qualification score ≥ 3.5/5)
□ Budget authority confirmed OR a CEO/CFO conversation is scheduled
□ Pain clearly articulated — write it in their exact words before opening this template
□ A proposal REVIEW MEETING is already booked — not "I'll look at it and get back to you"
```

**A proposal without a scheduled review meeting closes < 10% of the time.** Don't send speculatively.

### What We Build in 90 Days

**Weeks 1–3: Intelligence Layer**
ICP validation, hypothesis testing, signal configuration, database build. The system knows exactly who your buyer is and when to reach them — before your competitor does.

**Weeks 4–6: Activation Layer**
List building (200–500 target accounts), enrichment, lead scoring, email search, verification. Every contact enters the system scored and personalized.

**Weeks 7–9: Outreach Layer**
Multi-channel sequences launch: email + LinkedIn + cold call. Each sequence is triggered by a signal — not a calendar. Every email arrives at the right moment.

**Weeks 10–12: Pipeline + Optimize**
Reply routing, meeting automation, pipeline management. The system calibrates weekly based on what's working. By Day 90, it runs without constant oversight.

**After Day 90:** You own everything. Every list, sequence, scoring model, and piece of infrastructure lives on your platforms. The monthly retainer keeps one dedicated human SDR running the engine.
