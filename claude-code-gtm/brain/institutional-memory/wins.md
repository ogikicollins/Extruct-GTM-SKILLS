## What to Extract From Every Win

When a deal closes (Stage = Closed Won in `state.md`), the `deal-nurture` skill runs this analysis and adds an entry below.

### The 10 Win Dimensions

**1. ICP Score at Entry**
What was the company's lead score when it entered the pipeline? Was the model's prediction accurate?

**2. Trigger Signal**
Which buying signal originally triggered outreach? Which hypothesis matched?

**3. Entry Path**
How did this deal start — cold email, LinkedIn DM, signal-triggered, referral, inbound content?

**4. Persona**
Who was the primary contact — CRO, Scaling Founder, or New VP Sales?

**5. Timeline: First Touch → Close**
How many days from the first email to signed contract?

**6. Proof Point That Landed**
Which case study or stat appeared most in the winning sequence? What did the prospect quote back to us?

**7. Objections Raised and Resolved**
What objections came up? Which counters worked?

**8. The Tipping Point**
What specific moment or email or conversation moved them from "considering" to "yes"?

**9. Competitive Context**
Were they evaluating anyone else? Why did they choose SELLL?

**10. Buyer's Words**
What did they say when they decided to move forward? (Exact quote if possible — this becomes the best social proof)
