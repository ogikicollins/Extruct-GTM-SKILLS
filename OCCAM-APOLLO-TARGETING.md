# LIST HYGIENE RULES

1. **Never load the same contact twice.** Apollo tracks this — filter out "Already Contacted" before every export.
2. **Bounce rate above 8% = stop and fix your domain.** High bounces tank deliverability for every contact after.
3. **Tag every contact with their playbook.** `PB1`, `PB2`, `PB3A`, etc. CRM segmentation depends on it.
4. **Pull 50 before pulling 500.** Run the first 50 contacts through your sequence, check reply rates, then scale.
5. **Refresh PB3A and PB3B weekly.** These are time-sensitive signals. Stale trigger data = irrelevant emails.
6. **Archive no-reply contacts after Email 4.** Mark them `Closed - No Response` in Apollo. Reactivate in 90 days with a single re-entry email.
