## A/B Testing Instructions

- Split test each persona's subject lines 50/50 across your prospect list
- Run minimum 50 sends per variant before declaring a winner

## Personalization Upgrade Checklist

Before sending any email, add at least ONE of the following:

- [ ] Reference a specific LinkedIn post they wrote in the last 30 days
- [ ] Reference a specific company news event (funding, launch, hire, award)
- [ ] Name a tool you know they're using (Apollo, HubSpot, Salesforce, etc.)
- [ ] Reference a specific pain point visible in a job posting ("seeking SDR to build pipeline" → they don't have one yet)
- [ ] Name a competitor or adjacent company they'd recognize in a case study comparison
- [ ] Reference a specific metric from their website (number of customers, markets served, etc.)

Every additional personalization anchor adds approximately 1–2% to reply rate. Three anchors per email is the benchmark.
