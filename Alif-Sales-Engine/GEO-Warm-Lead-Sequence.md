## Why this converts
- **Touch 1 asks directly** — no "next email" tease. The lead already opted in; making them wait for the ask wastes the trust they've already given.
- **Touch 2 deliberately avoids a second meeting ask.** Two meeting requests back-to-back reads as pushy. Instead it offers a distinct, low-effort deliverable (an AI visibility snapshot) that re-engages non-responders without repeating the same CTA.
- **Touch 3 gives an easy off-ramp** ("skip the snapshot") that actually reframes toward the higher-intent ask — the meeting.
- **No named-competitor dependency** — the "someone else becomes the default answer" framing carries the same loss-aversion weight without requiring per-prospect research, so it scales cleanly across a mixed-ICP list.
