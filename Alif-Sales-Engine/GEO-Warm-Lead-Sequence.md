## Why this converts
- **Touch 1 asks directly** — no "next email" tease. The lead already opted in; making them wait for the ask wastes the trust they've already given.
- **Touch 2 deliberately avoids a second meeting ask.** Two meeting requests back-to-back reads as pushy. Instead it offers a distinct, low-effort deliverable (a named-competitor comparison) that re-engages non-responders without repeating the same CTA.
- **Touch 3 gives an easy off-ramp** ("skip the side-by-side") that actually reframes toward the higher-intent ask — the meeting — using the unclaimed touch 2 offer as social proof that there's something concrete to show up to.
